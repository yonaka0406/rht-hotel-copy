# Business Requirements Document

## Introduction

The rht-hotel Property Management System (PMS) is designed to streamline hotel operations, maximize revenue, and enhance guest experiences through comprehensive digital management capabilities. This document outlines the core business objectives, value propositions, and success metrics that drive the system's development and implementation.

## Business Objectives

### Primary Business Goals

#### Revenue Optimization
- **Objective:** Maximize hotel revenue through intelligent pricing, inventory management, and demand capture
- **Value Statement:** The system enables dynamic pricing strategies, captures unmet demand through waitlist management, and optimizes room allocation to increase overall revenue per available room (RevPAR)
- **Success Metrics:**
  - Increase in RevPAR by 15-25% through demand capture and optimization
  - Reduction in lost bookings due to unavailability by 20-30%
  - Improvement in average daily rate (ADR) through better pricing strategies

#### Operational Efficiency
- **Objective:** Reduce manual work and streamline hotel operations through automation and integration
- **Value Statement:** Automated processes, integrated systems, and intelligent workflows reduce staff workload and minimize human error
- **Success Metrics:**
  - Reduction in manual data entry by 60-80%
  - Decrease in reservation processing time by 50%
  - Improvement in staff productivity metrics

#### Guest Experience Enhancement
- **Objective:** Provide superior guest experiences through personalized service and seamless interactions
- **Value Statement:** Comprehensive guest profiles, preference tracking, and proactive communication create memorable experiences that drive loyalty and repeat business
- **Success Metrics:**
  - Increase in guest satisfaction scores by 20%
  - Improvement in repeat booking rates by 25%
  - Reduction in guest complaints by 40%

### Secondary Business Goals

#### Market Competitiveness
- **Objective:** Maintain competitive advantage through modern technology and superior service delivery
- **Value Statement:** Advanced PMS capabilities enable hotels to compete effectively with larger chains and online platforms
- **Business Constraints:** Must integrate with existing OTA channels and maintain compatibility with industry standards

#### Scalability and Growth
- **Objective:** Support business growth and expansion through scalable technology infrastructure
- **Value Statement:** System architecture supports multi-hotel operations and can scale with business growth
- **Business Constraints:** Must maintain performance standards as volume increases

## Business Value Propositions

### For Hotel Operators

#### Revenue Management
- **WHEN** rooms become available due to cancellations **THEN** the system **SHALL** automatically identify and notify waitlist guests to capture lost revenue
- **WHEN** demand patterns are analyzed **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide pricing recommendations to optimize revenue
- **IF** occupancy rates fall below targets **THEN** the system **SHALL** suggest promotional strategies

#### Operational Control
- **WHEN** reservations are created or modified **THEN** the system **SHALL** automatically update inventory and availability across all channels
- **WHEN** guest preferences are recorded **THEN** the system **SHALL** enable personalized service delivery
- **IF** operational issues arise **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide comprehensive audit trails for resolution

### For Hotel Staff

#### Workflow Efficiency
- **WHEN** staff access guest information **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide comprehensive profiles with history and preferences
- **WHEN** reservations require modifications **THEN** the system **SHALL** enable quick updates with automatic validation
- **IF** conflicts arise **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide clear resolution options and impact analysis

#### Decision Support
- **WHEN** staff need to make room assignments **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide availability and preference matching
- **WHEN** pricing decisions are needed **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide market data and recommendations
- **IF** guest issues occur **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide complete interaction history for context

### For Guests

#### Booking Experience
- **WHEN** guests search for availability **THEN** the system **SHALL** provide real-time, accurate information
- **WHEN** preferred rooms are unavailable **THEN** the system **SHALL** offer waitlist options with automatic notifications
- **IF** booking modifications are needed **THEN** the system **SHALL** enable flexible changes with clear policies

#### Service Personalization
- **WHEN** guests have established preferences **THEN** the system **SHALL** remember and apply them automatically
- **WHEN** guests return **THEN** the system **SHALL** recognize them and provide personalized service
- **IF** special requests are made **THEN** the system **SHALL** track and fulfill them consistently

## Feature-Specific Business Value

### Waitlist Management System

#### Business Problem
Hotels lose potential revenue when fully booked rooms turn away interested customers, especially when specific room preferences cannot be accommodated.

#### Business Solution
- **Revenue Recovery:** Capture 15-25% of lost bookings through effective waitlist management
- **Customer Retention:** Proactive communication builds loyalty even when immediate accommodation isn't available
- **Demand Intelligence:** Understand unmet demand patterns for capacity planning and pricing strategies

#### Success Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** 20-30% of waitlist entries convert to confirmed bookings
- **Revenue Impact:** $50,000+ annual revenue recovery per hotel through waitlist conversions
- **Customer Satisfaction:** 85%+ satisfaction rate for waitlist communication and service

### Client Relationship Management (CRM)
